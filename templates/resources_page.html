Scholarships

Many corporations, colleges and universities offer scholarships to high school students on FIRST teams. These scholarships show official recognition of the technical knowledge and life skills students have gained from participating in FIRST competitions.

  • FIRST alone has almost 900 individual scholarship opportunities with a total value of more than $19,000,000.
    All FIRST Scholarships can be found at this site.
  • There is also a FIRST Scholarship Newsletter that you can subscribe to for special information.
  • There are many scholarship opportunities out there for STEM and non-STEM majors.
    This page is a resource for scholarships currently available and any other new scholarships our team learns of, so check back often!
    Scholarship America  - a link to a lot of different scholarships available for all different kinds of majors
